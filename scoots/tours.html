A Guide to the Beaches of Cozumel

PALANCAR BEACH

Located 20 kilometers south of San Miguel Cozumel's town center, it is possibly the most popular beach in Cozumel. Palacar Beach is easily accessible through a road that takes you from the main highway to the beautiful white sand beach. Here, you will also find a beach club with a restaurant, bar, sunbeds, and various activities.

EL CIELO BEACH

Absolute must-visit. Hundreds of starfish covering the white ocean floor make this beach and off-coast sandbank one of the most memorable sites on the island. El Cielo can only be reached by boat, either a private vessel with the proper permits or a guided tour.

ISLA DE PASION BEACH

Uninhabited and just one kilometer long, this gorgeous island is one of the most coveted spots in Cozumel. It sits just off the north coast and is part of a wildlife refuge.
