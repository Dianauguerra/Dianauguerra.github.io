FAQ

What kind of driver’s license can I use?

We accept a current and valid driver’s license from your home country with the proper class or endorsement permitting you to ride the motorcycle, or equivalent of the motorcycle, you are renting.

How old do you have to be to make a reservation for a rental?

The minimun age to make a reservation is 18 years. Customers that are 18-24 years old are required to have the Platinum Insurance with their reservation.

Is there a required deposit to make a reservation?

You can make a reservation online with no deposit necessary. The deposit will be required the same day of pick up. The amount of deposit is 30% of the total amount.

What kind of damage insurance does Scoots offer?

We offer a theft and damage waiver. It completely covers damage and partially covers thef. The renter pays $0 per damage and up to $1000 per theft occurrence. Contact our agents for more information.

Are customers welcomed as walk-ins?

Both locations are open to walk-ins during their respective business hours.
