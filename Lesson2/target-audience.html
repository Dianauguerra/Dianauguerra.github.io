Description of target audience:

The target audience are residents and tourists that are or will be in the area. They are people who like to plan out their days and need realiable information.

Two Personas:

#1

Name: Raquel

Age: 18

Occupation: Student

Family status: single

Interests: Being outdoors around nature and doing community service

#2

Name: John

Age: 45

Occupation: Teacher

Family status: Married with kids

Interests: Spending time with family in road trips and trekking with friends

Scenarios:

  • Student is looking to do community service as a project for school. She needs a reliable place and contacts.
  • A family is visiting a family member in the area. They want to know the weather for the weekend and any events to attend as a family.
  • A school teacher wants to program a field trip but wants to know the weather to assign a day and time.
  • A group of teenagers are in spring break and are looking for local activities such as free concerts and spring events.
  • Elderly woman wants to get information of local bingo events.
